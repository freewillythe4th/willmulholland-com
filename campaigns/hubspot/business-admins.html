Blinq for HubSpot just got a major upgrade

Hi there,

Wherever your team meets someone in real life, they can capture the contact in seconds. It lands in HubSpot already enriched and ready for follow-up before your team leaves the room.


Until now, the in-person side of your relationships has lived outside of your CRM. Today, Blinq is live on the HubSpot App Marketplace with a native integration built for events, partner meetings, and everything in between. Every contact your team meets is already working for you in HubSpot.

What's better

  • Real-time sync: contacts hit HubSpot the moment your team captures them
  • AI Enrichment: missing details are filled in before the contact reaches HubSpot, including email, phone, LinkedIn, company, and more
  • AI Notetaker summaries: conversation context saves to the contact record so your whole team has the full picture
  • Custom field mapping: map Blinq fields to your HubSpot properties so contacts land exactly where your team needs them
  • Clear attribution: contacts are tagged by rep, campaign, and source, ready for the sequences and reporting your team already uses
