The practical bits

Questions worth asking.

I already use Claude. What changes?

A blank model makes you choose and explain the process. Intelligent Growth selects a defined product marketing method for the job and applies it to the context you provide.

What can I use for free?

Free access includes the positioning diagnosis and the competitive gap workflow. The diagnosis identifies the current market frame, biggest risk, missing evidence and next research action. A full positioning recommendation, messaging review, launch plan and the rest of the catalogue requires membership.

How does the 7-day free trial work?

New members can use every paid method in Intelligent Growth OS for seven days. A payment card is required. The trial converts to A$49 a month unless you cancel before the trial ends. The annual option remains A$490 a year and does not use this trial.

Is this my Marketing Brain?

No. Your Marketing Brain is the private context and infrastructure you own. Intelligent Growth is the subscribed library of maintained marketing methods that can use the context you choose to provide. If you want that context shared across a team, cited and access-scoped, that is the Team Brain.

Does it replace my judgment?

No. Challenge the recommendation, check the assumptions and make the final call. The product gives you a stronger first pass and makes the thinking visible.

Does it expose the underlying methods or source material?

No. Protected methods run on the hosted service. Your chosen client receives the applied result for the current job, not private method files, reference libraries, provider prompts or source documents.

Can it teach me how to do the work?

Yes. It can explain the relevant principles, walk you through the process, show why a recommendation holds and help apply the method to your situation. It will not reproduce or export the private source library.

What context should I give it?

Start with the product, audience, alternatives, customer evidence, business constraint and deliverable you need. If something important is missing, the result should tell you.

What happens to my data?

The product needs the context you send for the current task. Analytics record safe events such as connection, method, tier, client, outcome and broad error category. They do not record prompts, outputs, email addresses, credentials, source text or full URLs. Read the privacy policy for the complete terms.

Why does part of the work run on your model API?

MCP does not require this. It is a product choice. Selected methods and quality checks run on the hosted service so the deeper process can be applied consistently without sending the full private method to your model. The structured result returns to the client you chose.

Will it work in ChatGPT?

Full custom MCP setup is currently available on ChatGPT web for eligible Business, Enterprise and Edu workspaces. An admin or owner must enable developer mode and create the app. Pro accounts currently have read and fetch connector support only.

What if setup does not work?

Use the troubleshooting steps or message Will on WhatsApp. Include the client and the step that failed. Never send an access token or private company content.
