Why it is safe to share

Default is deny. Out of scope means invisible, not just locked.

Access is enforced in the server, not by asking the model to behave. If you have not granted someone a folder, those notes never show up in their search, their lists or their roll-ups. They cannot see that the file exists.

The rest holds the line too: it stays inside the vault, skips dotfiles and secrets, redacts anything key-shaped, and writes every read to an audit log tagged with who asked. It is never on the public internet, only reachable over your private connection.

The demo people remember is the denial: a scoped teammate asks for the private folder, and the server refuses in front of the room. That is the moment a shared brain becomes safe to hand out.

The practical bits

Questions worth asking.

How is this different from the Intelligent Growth methods?

The methods are the how-to for marketing work: positioning, messaging, launches. This is your team's own context, made answerable and access-scoped. Use both together. The methods do the thinking, the team brain remembers what your company knows.

Is this the Marketing Brain?

It is the Marketing Brain made shareable. The cohort teaches you to build the brain as files you own. The Team Memory MCP serves those files to your whole team, with cited answers and per-person access, so one person's brain becomes everyone's.

Where does my data live?

On your own machine or network, reached over your private connection. It never goes to the public internet, and nothing trains on it.

Can one person see another person's notes?

Only if you grant it. Default is deny, and out-of-scope notes never appear in that person's search, lists or roll-ups. They cannot even see that the file exists.

Does it ever make things up?

No. It answers with the note and the date, or it says it does not know. That is the whole reason you can trust it in front of a customer.

What do I connect it to?

Any folder of markdown notes. The Marketing Brain starter, an Obsidian vault, or a git repo all work. If your notes are somewhere else, message me and we will sort it.

Which clients does it work in?

Claude, Claude Code, and eligible ChatGPT Business, Enterprise and Edu workspaces with custom MCP enabled. Each person connects once.
