Quick answers

Before you sign up

Is it really free?

Yes. No card, no catch, and no IT tickets, you just need your own ChatGPT or Claude login. Register on Luma and the calendar invite with the join link lands in your inbox.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. You describe what you want, the AI builds it. You'll watch it happen on screen.

Do I need to use the terminal?

No. You can do all of this just by talking to the AI, no terminal and no code. The terminal makes you faster once you're comfortable, but you don't need it to start.

Why is my AI output always generic?

Because the chat box forgets who you are. The fix isn't a cleverer prompt, it's giving the AI your context once, your brand, your customer, the way you write, so it stops sounding generic. That's what we build. You shouldn't have to tell anyone it came from AI. If it's useful, it's useful.

Where does this live, and how does it run without me?

It's just files in a folder. No app to host, no website to build. The AI handles the technical plumbing, you don't memorise a single command. Once it's built it can run on a schedule and send you the result, so it works while you don't.

Is it safe to point AI at my work?

Yes, with a bit of care. The thing to avoid is leaving something exposed, like a password or a key. I'll show you how to keep what you build safe, so you leave knowing how to work without putting yourself at risk.

Can I use this on sensitive or confidential company data?

Yes, and it's the question I get most from in-house marketers. You decide what the AI sees. The simplest move is to anonymise sensitive docs first, swap real names and numbers for placeholders, so the system learns the shape of your work without the confidential details. The business tiers of Claude and ChatGPT don't train on what you put in, and if you need to go further you can run a local model that scrubs the private details before anything leaves your machine. In the workshop I'll show you where to draw that line.

Is this the whole course?

No. This one builds the foundation, your context and your first reusable skill, start to finish. The full brain, the operating setup and the rest of the skills, is a later workshop and the cohort.

Is it recorded?

Yes, but the recording only goes to people who show up live. The whole point is building with you in real time and answering your questions on the spot, so block out the hour and be there. Bring a marketing problem you want solved.

Is this just a pitch for the cohort?

No. The hour is the build. There's a paid cohort later and I'll mention it once, but you walk away with a working skill and the steps to make your own whether you ever pay me a cent or not.

Who's running it?
